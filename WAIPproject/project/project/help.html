Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket?

You can book tickets through our website or mobile app by selecting your departure and destination cities, travel date, and number of passengers.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Yes, tickets can be cancelled up to 24 hours before departure. Cancellation fees may apply depending on when you cancel.

How do I change my booking?

You can modify your booking through the 'My Tickets' section after logging into your account. Changes are subject to availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and select mobile payment options.
